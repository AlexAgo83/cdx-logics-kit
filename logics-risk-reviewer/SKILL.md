# Risk review

## For backlog items

- Add a short `Risks` subsection under `# Notes`:
  - Risk
  - Mitigation
  - Dependencies

## For tasks

- Add a `# Risks & rollback` section when the change is risky:
  - What can break
  - How to detect regressions
  - How to rollback safely
